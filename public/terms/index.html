Information only

House Checker is intended as an informational tool to help buyers review asking prices, local market context, and possible repair costs. It does not provide formal valuation, mortgage, legal, or surveying advice.

No guarantee of outcome

Property negotiations depend on many factors, including competition, timing, condition, survey findings, and seller expectations. Use the calculator as a guide rather than a guaranteed basis for an accepted offer.

External sources

Some site features rely on external property data sources. Availability, completeness, and accuracy may change over time, and those services may publish their own terms of use.

Your responsibility

You remain responsible for checking any important figures, seeking professional advice where needed, and deciding whether a property offer is appropriate for your circumstances.
