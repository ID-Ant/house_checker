What House Negotiator is designed to do

House Negotiator helps UK buyers sense-check an asking price before they submit an offer or counter-offer. Instead of relying only on instinct, it brings together sold-price context, local market averages, and the likely cost of work needed at the property.

The calculator is intended to support better preparation. It can help you identify a realistic starting point for negotiation, especially when you are comparing a home against nearby market conditions and visible repair requirements.

Who it is for

  • First-time buyers who want a clearer framework before making an offer.
  • Home movers comparing an asking price against local market evidence.
  • Buyers reviewing repair costs before deciding on a revised bid.
  • Anyone who wants a structured house offer calculator focused on UK property data.

What it does not replace

  • A full survey or specialist structural advice.
  • Independent mortgage or legal guidance.
  • Professional valuation advice for unusual or high-risk properties.
  • Your own judgement about local demand, competition, and urgency.

Why this matters for buyers

Property negotiations move quickly, and buyers often need a fast view of whether a seller's price feels reasonable. A calculator cannot decide the right number for every transaction, but it can reduce guesswork and give you a more defensible position when discussing price.
